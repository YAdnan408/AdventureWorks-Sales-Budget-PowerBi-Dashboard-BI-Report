AdventureWorks Sales & Budget Dashboard
An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing sales performance, budget variance, and product trends for AdventureWorks — a multinational bicycle company.

Dataset

AdventureWorks_Database.xlsx — 58,189 sales transactions across 5 tables (Sales, Customers, Products, Calendar, Territories)
Budget.xlsx — Monthly 2023 budget targets by product and category

What the Dashboard Shows

Total Revenue, Budget, Variance, and Variance % as KPI cards
Sales vs. Budget comparison by product category
Monthly sales trend and variance for 2023
Top products and customers by revenue

Key Findings

Total revenue of $29.31M across 2021–2023, with 2023 being the strongest year at $16.47M
Bikes account for 96.6% of all revenue, led by the Mountain-200 series
US and Australia together contribute 62.9% of global revenue
Monthly 2023 sales grew consistently from $887K in January to $1.95M in December

Tools Used
Power BI · Power Query (M) · DAX · Microsoft Excel
